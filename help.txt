clr [clear the screen] [takes no arguements]
ls [list files and directories in the current directory] [takes no arguements]
pwd [present working directory] [takes no arguements]
cd [change to a directory] [takes a path as input to change to it]
echo [print to screen] [takes a statement as input]
pause [pauses the system untill enter is pressed] [takes no arguements]
quit [break out of the system] [takes no arguements]
environ [lists the environment variables] [takes no arguements]
